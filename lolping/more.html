The League of Legends Ping-Discord bot!

If you've played League of Legends, you might have noticed that the "Enemy is missing" Ping has another purpose than just saying that your enemy laner is gone.

This wonderful ping that comes in form of a questionmark, can be also applied as a reaction to your teams or friends rather questioning actions that they sometimes take.


Now you can do it in Discord!

?ping @userName
